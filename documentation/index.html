Hospital Management System

Advanced management software for hospital, clinic

  • version: 1.0
  • created: 07/15/2015
  • by: Abhivendra Singh

This software is designed for the ABV-Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management Gwalior's Clinic to help them manage the patient record.

Key Features

Advanced, powerful, flexible complete management software for hospital. It encourages paper-less job at hospital, hence contributing towards a greener environment.
Integrates and facilitates all user area of a hospital :
  • Administrator
  • Doctor
  • Registration
  • Pharmacist
 
 

Administrator -

  • Manage departments of hospital
  • Manage doctor account
  • Manage registration account
  • Manage pharmacist account
  • Keep records of hospitals stock medicines and status
  • Monitor hospital events
    • watch availability of doctors, registers and pharmacists
    • manage employees leave status
    • manage medicines inventory
  • Watch system log of user interactions

 

Doctor -

  • View the patient queue
  • Prescribe tests
  • Create prescription for patient
  • Keep record of patient case history
  • Refer patient to different doctor of the hospital
  • Cross Refer a patient to different hospital or doctor
  • Keep record of his own history
  • Manage own profile

Terminologies Used for Medicine Schedule

  • BIS- Twice Daily
  • DA- Every Other Day
  • BT- At Bed Time
  • OM- Every Morning
  • 1OD- One Everyday
  • 3OD- Three Everyday
  • ON- Every Night
  • OPD- Once per Day
  • PO- by Mouth Orally
  • SA- Use your Judgement
  • STAT- Immediately
  • SUPP- Suppository
  • SOS- If Needed
  • QQN- Every four hour
  • UD- As Directed

Registration -

  • Manage patient. account opening and updating
  • Assigning patient to specific doctor
  • View availability of doctors
  • View registered patients
  • Manage own profile

Pharmacist -

  • View prescription given by doctor
  • Provide medication to prescriptions
  • View history of prescriptions
  • View stock medicines status and store
  • Manage own profile
